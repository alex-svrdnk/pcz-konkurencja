Our Menu

Tacos al Pastor

Traditional Mexican tacos with marinated pork, cooked on a vertical spit, served with pineapple, onions, and cilantro in a soft corn tortilla.

Chiles Rellenos

Roasted chili peppers stuffed with cheese or meat, dipped in an egg batter and fried until golden, served with tomato sauce.

Enchiladas Verdes

Corn tortillas filled with chicken or cheese, smothered in green tomatillo sauce, baked with melted cheese, and served with avocado and sour cream.

Pozole

Traditional soup made from hominy corn simmered with pork or chicken, seasoned with chili and spices, served with radishes, cabbage, and lime.

Mole Poblano

Chicken stewed in a rich sauce made from chocolate and chili, with hints of cinnamon, nuts, and spices, served with rice and corn tortillas.
